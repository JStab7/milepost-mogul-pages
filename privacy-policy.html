Milepost Mogul

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 30, 2026

Milepost Mogul is an offline single-player tycoon game.

Data Collection

Milepost Mogul does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

The game stores local progress on your device, including cash, reputation, upgrades, completed missions, settings, and save time. This save data stays on your device and is not sent to the developer or any third party.

Tracking

Milepost Mogul does not track users across apps or websites.

Third Parties

Milepost Mogul does not include third-party advertising, analytics, social SDKs, external accounts, or in-app purchases in this release.

Permissions

Milepost Mogul does not request access to location, camera, microphone, contacts, photos, health data, Bluetooth, or other sensitive device permissions.

Children

Milepost Mogul does not knowingly collect personal information from children or adults.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the support contact provided on the App Store product page.
