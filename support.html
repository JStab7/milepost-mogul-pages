Milepost Mogul

Support

Support information for the App Store release candidate.

Common Questions

Does the game require internet?

No. Milepost Mogul is designed as an offline single-player game.

Does the game have ads or in-app purchases?

No. This release has no ads, in-app purchases, subscriptions, or external account system.

How do I reset progress?

Open Settings inside the game and choose Reset local save.

Where is progress stored?

Progress is stored locally on the device.

Support Contact

Use the Support URL on the App Store product page to return to this page. When asking for help, include your device model, iOS version, and a short description of what happened.

Milepost Mogul stores progress locally on your device, so the developer cannot recover deleted local saves.
